About Lyrus

Disrupting the traditional CRDO landscape through high-complexity generic formulations and uncompromising execution.

Our Mission

To engineer highly specialized, complex pharmaceutical formulations that traditional CDMOs avoid. We specialize in turning highly hydrophobic or unstable molecules into viable, FDA-compliant commercial products.

Our commitment to 21 CFR Part 11 compliance and cGMP standards ensures that our data packages sail through global regulatory scrutiny.

The Lyrus Advantage

We operate an agile, purely R&D-focused model. By leveraging our 11 global CMO partners, we are not bogged down by manufacturing capacity constraints. Our scientists focus 100% of their energy on formulation and analytical perfection.
