# Products and Services: Lyrus Life Sciences

Lyrus functions as a full-spectrum research and development partner for the pharmaceutical industry, and is also involved in the development and trading of pharmaceutical products.

## Services Offered
1. **Pre-formulation Studies:** 
   Conducting comprehensive studies to ensure robust dosage forms, minimize development costs, and improve success rates.
2. **Formulation Development:** 
   Expertise in developing various dosage forms, including oral solids, liquids, powders, granules, pellets, topicals, parenterals, and sprays.
3. **Analytical Development:** 
   Comprehensive analytical method development and validation.
4. **Regulatory Services:** 
   Compiling product dossiers and responding to regulatory queries to support global market entry.
5. **Manufacturing Support:** 
   Capabilities ranging from pilot manufacturing to exhibit and commercial manufacturing support at its cGMP-equipped facilities.

## Products & Therapeutic Areas
While primarily a service-oriented R&D organization, Lyrus has developed a diverse portfolio of generic and specialty formulations, as well as nutraceutical and OTC (over-the-counter) products.

**Therapeutic Segments:**
- Nervous System
- Cardiovascular System
- Diabetes and Endocrinology
- Urology
- Gastroenterology
- Pain Management
- Anti-infectives

**Dosage Forms:**
- **Tablets:** e.g., Methenamine Hippurate, Metformin PR, Sucralfate
- **Capsules:** e.g., Nitrofurantoin
- **Others:** Soft gels, sachets, liquid orals, sprays, injectables, ophthalmics, external preparations (creams, gels).
