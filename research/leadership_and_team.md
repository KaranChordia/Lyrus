The leadership team at Lyrus Life Sciences consists of experienced veterans in the pharmaceutical industry:

- **Rajesh Goel:** *Chief Executive Officer (CEO)*
  He has over 15 years of experience in the pharmaceutical industry, having previously worked with Teva and Apotex, and is a qualified CA, CS, and Cost Accountant.
  
- **C.P. Bothra:** *Chairman*
  A veteran in the pharmaceutical industry with over 40 years of experience. He is the founder and former Managing Director of Medreich Ltd.
  
- **Hemanth Kumar Bothra:** *Managing Director*
  Co-founder of Lyrus Life Sciences, bringing 17 years of experience in the pharmaceutical industry.
  
- **Leela Kanwar:** *Co-Founder and Director*
  
- **Uma Maheshwari:** *Vice President – Global Compliance*
  She has 24 years of experience in the pharma industry, specializing in quality assurance, regulatory affairs, pharmacovigilance, and technology transfer.
