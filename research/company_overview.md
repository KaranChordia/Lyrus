# Company Overview: Lyrus Life Sciences

Lyrus Life Sciences is a research-driven, global specialty pharmaceutical company headquartered in Bengaluru, India. Founded in 2016, the company operates primarily as a Contract Research and Development Organization (CRDO) and is focused on developing differentiated pharmaceutical products and innovative drug delivery solutions.

## Core Identity
- **Headquarters:** Bengaluru, India
- **Founded:** 2016
- **Business Model:** Contract Research and Development Organization (CRDO) & Specialty Pharmaceuticals

## Core Competencies
1. **Research & Development:** 
   The company maintains a strong R&D infrastructure with a dedicated team of scientists focused on pre-formulation, formulation development, and analytical development.
2. **Drug Delivery Technologies:** 
   Lyrus is known for its expertise in novel drug delivery systems, including nanotechnology-based topicals and base modifications to enhance bioavailability.
3. **Manufacturing Services:** 
   The company offers pilot and exhibit manufacturing, as well as commercial manufacturing support, to help clients bring drug products to the global marketplace at its cGMP-equipped facilities.
4. **Regulatory Support:** 
   Lyrus provides regulatory services, such as compiling product dossiers and responding to regulatory queries, to assist in the approval and compliance processes for pharmaceutical products in global markets.

## Global Presence
Lyrus collaborates with global pharmaceutical firms and distributes products through partnerships. It has a significant presence in export markets such as Australia, the United Kingdom, Europe, and South Africa.
