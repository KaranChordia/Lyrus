# Talk Track (In-Person): Chairman Pitch

## Opening (30 seconds)
Mr. Bothra, thank you for your time. I looked at Lyrus as a business that wins in complex development and regulated-market execution. My proposal is simple: I want to help Lyrus grow more safely and execute faster by strengthening three things: distributor-facing credibility, milestone visibility, and documentation discipline.

## The Three Chairman-Level Priorities (2 minutes)
1. Growth without dependence on a single channel.
2. Scale execution speed without risking quality.
3. Strengthen regulated-market credibility with proof-driven communication.

## What I Offer (3 minutes)
### 1) Content, Website, and Digital Presence Management
I will run a consistent communication engine: case studies, capability brochure/deck refresh, website creation/updates, and ongoing communication management (LinkedIn/newsletter/PR-style updates) that builds trust in regulated markets.

### 2) Internal Workflows and Operating Dashboards
I will build simple internal dashboards that teams actually use daily: milestones, stability tracking, document status, handoffs, and partner (CMO) status, so execution becomes consistent and visible.

### 3) AI Agents (Controlled and Auditable)
I will deploy tightly-scoped AI assistants that remove repetitive work: dossier drafting assistants, QA batch-record checkers, deviation/CAPA summarizers, regulatory update briefs, and knowledge-base organization. Humans remain in control for approval.

## How We Start (1 minute)
I propose a 30-day pilot. In that month you will see tangible outputs: a case study, a brochure/deck refresh, website improvements, a working internal workflow dashboard prototype, and one narrow AI assistant pilot with clear boundaries. After that, we decide whether to continue as a combined retainer.

## What I Need from You (30 seconds)
Minimal time from two owners: one for BD collateral review and one for execution workflow review, about one hour per week each. We can work with anonymized documents initially, and nothing goes external until you approve.

## Close (15 seconds)
If you approve, I can start this month and bring back a clear 90-day roadmap with scope and costs after the pilot.

## Likely Questions and Simple Answers
- Why a retainer and not one-off work?
  A retainer creates continuity: collateral, systems, and processes improve each month, and your team gets a stable operating rhythm.
- Will this distract scientists/QA?
  The pilot is designed to reduce interruptions: short weekly reviews and practical deliverables that remove repeat work.
- How do you handle confidentiality?
  We can start with anonymized samples; then move to controlled access with whatever restrictions you require.
